Getting here

Travel & Lodging

Nearest Airport

Albany International (ALB), about 45 minutes from the venue. Stewart International (SWF) is a further but sometimes cheaper alternative.

Hotel Room Block

The Ridgeline Hotel — mention "Alex & Jordan Wedding" or use code AJ2026. Block held until September 10.

Getting Around

A shuttle runs between the Ridgeline Hotel and the venue Saturday from 3:00–11:30 PM. Rideshares are reliable in the area otherwise.

What to Pack

Expect crisp fall weather, 45–60°F. Ceremony and cocktail hour are partly outdoors — a light jacket is a good idea.

Good to know

FAQ

What's the dress code?

Cocktail attire. The ceremony and cocktail hour are partly outdoors, so consider layers and comfortable shoes for grass.

Are kids welcome?

We love your little ones, but we've decided to make this an adults-only celebration, with the exception of immediate family.

Is there parking at the venue?

Yes, free parking is available on-site, but we recommend the shuttle from the hotel to avoid the walk from the overflow lot.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Invitations will indicate if a plus-one is included. If you're unsure, just reach out to us directly.
